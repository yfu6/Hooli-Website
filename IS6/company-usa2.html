Fast Enterprises

Company Profile

Fast Enterprises is an industry leader in the development and installation of software for government agencies. In 1998 we forever changed the way government revenue agencies support their business with the introduction of GenTax®. This commercial off the shelf (COTS) solution has enabled many agencies to take advantage of package implementations, providing more cost efficient and fully functional software systems to our clients. We have expanded beyond tax and now do implementations for Motor Vehicle, Drivers License, Unemployment Insurance and Unemployment Benefit agencies.


Advantages of Employment

  • FAST has received recognition from Glassdoor six years in a row and has ranked in two different areas. FAST has repeatedly been selected as one of the Best Places to Work since 2014. Our employees’ work ethic and ability to collaborate creates a family feel and is the reason we are among the top places to work.
